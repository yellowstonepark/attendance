Taking Attendance with Zoom

The Difficulties

Unfortunately, you cannot copy and paste participants in Zoom, unless you join on the browser. With the attendance program, you can take attendance with speed and ease!

The Solution

It is advised to have a student do the attendance for you, and just give them this tutorial.

Follow these steps:

  1. In the first textbox, you must paste the students in your class.
    1. Go to Google Classroom
    2. Pick your class
    3. Click the word "People" at the top of the page
    4. Highlight and copy all the students in the class
    5. Paste it into the first textbox

      6. ***Note: Each name MUST be on a separte line or the program will not work.

  2. In the second textbox, you must paste the participants.
    1. Click on the Zoom link
    2. Instead of joining with the program, click the button (in the middle, and on the bottom) that says join on your browser
    3. Join the meeting on the browser
    4. Open up the participants tab (button in the middle, all the way on the bottom)
    5. Highlight all the participants (or just click Ctrl + A) and copy them to your clipboard
    6. Paste them into the second textbox
  3. Now just click "Run Attendance"!

Now that you have clicked the button, you will see at the bottom a list of who is present and who is absent (each separated by " * ").

Enjoy!
